When to Visit Pediatric Urgent Care

Not every situation requires an emergency room visit. You should bring your child to our pediatric urgent care in Clear Lake if they experience any of the following:

High Fever That Doesn’t Respond to Medication: When your child’s temperature stays high even after medication, it could indicate an underlying infection. Our medical team quickly assesses the cause and provides effective treatment to bring relief.

Persistent Cough or Sore Throat: A lingering cough or sore throat may be a sign of a bacterial or viral infection. We examine your child and recommend the right care to help them recover comfortably.

Ear Pain or Drainage: Ear infections are common in children and can be very painful. Our experienced providers diagnose and treat ear conditions promptly to prevent complications.

Mild Asthma Attacks: If your child experiences shortness of breath or wheezing, our team provides safe and fast relief for mild asthma flare-ups to help them breathe easier.

Nausea, Vomiting, or Diarrhea: Digestive issues can quickly lead to dehydration in children. We provide gentle, effective care to restore comfort and hydration.

Minor Cuts or Injuries That Need Stitches: Our clinic handles small wounds and lacerations efficiently with minimal discomfort, ensuring proper healing and scar care.

Allergic Reactions Such as Hives or Swelling: Allergic symptoms can develop suddenly. We identify the trigger and provide quick treatment to ease discomfort and prevent worsening reactions.

When symptoms appear suddenly, our urgent care Clear Lake location is ready to help. We treat patients of all ages, including infants and adolescents, and we provide after-hours care for busy families who can’t always schedule pediatrician appointments during the week.
